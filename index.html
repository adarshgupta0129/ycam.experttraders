Motion Sensor /
Pet Friendly Motion Sensor
The Motion Sensor picks up movement when the
Y-cam Protect Alarm is activated. Ideal for protecting
large areas and rooms, it detects motion up to 12m
away and has a detection zone of 90 degrees. It also
includes a tamper-proof switch to prevent
unauthorised removal. For best results place the
sensor 2.3m from the ground, with a clear field of view.
You can turn off the motion sensors by activating Home
Mode, which keeps the Protect alarm system on but
allows you to move freely around your home knowing
the Access sensors will be triggered if there is any
unauthorised entry.
